Capybara

Capybaras are native to South America and are the world's largest rodent. They are reside in water and also on land since they are semi-aquatic mammals. They are social animals that live in groups and are known for their calm and friendly demeanor. Capybaras are herbivores that eat grasses, aquatic plants and vegetation from their environments.
